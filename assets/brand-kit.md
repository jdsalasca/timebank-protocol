## Usage Rules

- Preserve icon balance and spacing.
- Use high contrast for accessibility.
- Keep trust-focused subtitles concise.
- Prefer dark background for announcement cards.

## Suggested Placements

- Repository social preview card.
- Protocol docs homepage.
- Community pilot announcements.
